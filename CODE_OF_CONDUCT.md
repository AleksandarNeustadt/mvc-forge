# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want collaboration around MVC Forge to be respectful, direct, and focused on building a reliable framework.

Examples of expected behavior:

- communicate constructively and assume good intent,
- give actionable technical feedback,
- document decisions and trade-offs clearly,
- respect privacy, credentials, and private infrastructure details.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language,
- publishing private repository content, credentials, or customer data,
- intentionally bypassing security controls or review processes.

## Enforcement

Repository owners may restrict access, close discussions, or remove collaborators when behavior harms project safety or team productivity.
